Mechanic outline:

Enemy:

Will face the player
when player within range (stable by variable) will shoot one of three random balls in the direction they're facing
Each ball will announce the variable "EnhancedDodge" is true when whitin a certain range of the player, and turn it to false if not within distance
The balls are named "leftDodgeAttack", "rightDodgeAttack", and "jumpDodgeAttack"
If the ball withing range is leftDodgeAttack then it'll announce "dodgeDirection" is equal to left, and viceversa for the other balls
balls disappear after travelling a certain distance or hitting player


Player:

- First person
- When pressing a "WASD" direction button and space the player will dash a small distance in that direction. if pressing space without a directional key, the player will jump
- If the player dodges, using the correct directional input based on announced variable "dodgeDirection", then the player animation, enemy animation, enemies shot balls speed, and just overall physics speed will decrease to a quarter for 2 seconds (adjustable)
